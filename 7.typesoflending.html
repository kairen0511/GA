Types of Lending we offer:

  1. Personal Loans: For personal expenses like debt consolidation or home improvements. Fees: 2% processing fee.
  2. Business Loans: For small and medium-sized business needs like working capital or expansion. Fees: 1.5% service charge.
  3. Real Estate Loans: For purchasing or developing residential and commercial properties. Fees: 3% application fee.
  4. Invoice Financing: For businesses to obtain funds against outstanding invoices. Fees: 2.5% of the invoice value.
  5. Student Loans: For covering education-related expenses. Fees: 1% origination fee.
